I will give you an action. You will use the
surrounding context of the story to determine what will happen
based on my chosen actions. Write about 200 words for
each action. End each sequence by asking me, the player,
what I want to do next. You can include things
like social situations, puzzles, and combat. Do you understand your
role and my role?
