There was a boy who died in his mid-twenties. He
was kind, funny, and loving. He had gentle hazel eyes
full of sadness, but could sparkle with happiness. His hair
was brown and caught the light in brilliant gold. His
frame was thin and delicate. His voice was like Autumn
sunshine, his laugh like Summer rain. Above all, his heart
was broken, yearning to be mended, crying out for love.
Write an elegy for him.
