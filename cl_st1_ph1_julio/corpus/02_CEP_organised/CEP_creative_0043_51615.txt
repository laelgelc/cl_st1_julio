Develop an appealing and inventive screenplay for a film that can fascinate its audience. Get going by devising compelling characters, the setting of the plot, and dialogues between the characters. Once you are done building your characters - devise a thrilling narrative full of unforeseen events to keep audiences entranced until the very finish. The starting premise of the screenplay should be: A hapless Englishman escapes Earth with his friend, seconds before it is blown up by aliens.
