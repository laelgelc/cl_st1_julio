In research methods, a variable is any characteristic or property
that can be manipulated, measured, or controlled in research. Variables
are used to test a relationship between two or more
concepts, or to assess the effect of one or more
independent variables on a dependent variable. The main types of
variables in a study include independent variables, dependent variables, confounding
variables, and exogenous variables.
